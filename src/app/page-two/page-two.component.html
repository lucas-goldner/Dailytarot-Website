What is Daily Tarot

Tarot cards are unlike popular belief not for fortune telling. In Daily Tarot you will receive
every day a new tarot card, which has a description. By following the description you
could notice different things happening in your surroundings or get comfortable with a new
kind of energy. At the end of the day you can go write what happened on that day.
Over time you will grow a collection of events, which could influence you in a special way.
